🌴 Discovering Sri Lanka’s Cultural Triangle

Sri Lanka’s Cultural Triangle connects the ancient cities of Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, and the majestic Sigiriya rock fortress. These UNESCO World Heritage sites offer a window into a 2000-year-old civilization. Climb Sigiriya at sunrise, visit cave temples in Dambulla, and marvel at royal ruins in Polonnaruwa. Guided tours can reveal hidden frescoes and stories lost to time.

🏞️ Train Ride Through the Tea Hills of Ella

The scenic train ride from Kandy to Ella is among the most beautiful railway journeys globally. Passing lush green tea plantations, cascading waterfalls, and misty mountains, the ride offers unforgettable views. Stop at Ella town to hike Little Adam’s Peak and visit Nine Arches Bridge.

🏖️ Beaches of Mirissa: Sun, Surf, and Serenity

Mirissa is a slice of paradise on Sri Lanka’s southern coast. With golden sands and turquoise waters, it’s perfect for swimming, surfing, and whale watching. At night, beachside shacks serve fresh seafood with a backdrop of twinkling stars.
